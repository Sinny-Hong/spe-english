Level 2 句意翻譯理解

( ) 3. Hey, guys, can we stop by here?
(A) 嘿，大家，我們可以在這裡站著等嗎？
(B) 嘿，大家，我們可以順路停留這裏嗎？
(C) 嘿，大家，我們在這裡要出發了嗎？
( ) 4. I bought it through an online marketplace.
(A) 我是透過網路購物平台買這張票的。
(B) 我是在售票處排隊搶買這張票的。
(C) 這張票是朋友上網送給我的驚喜。
( ) 5. but there were also some fake deals and scams.
(A) 網路上以前到處都在舉辦免費大贈送活動。
(B) 雖然拍賣網站非常的安全完全不會有問題。
(C) 但網路上也存在一些虛假交易和騙局詐騙。
( ) 6. The most popular ones were useful for getting tickets.
(A) 最受歡迎的那些物品在搶票時是非常有用的。
(B) 最貴的門票對歌迷來說，通常是沒有任何好處的。
(C) 買最熱門的門票對學生來說，是极度危險的事。

Level 3 翻譯挑戰

( ) 7. 他是我最喜歡的韓國歌手。他是世界上最好的歌手！
(A) My favorite Korean singer's. He's the best singer in the world!
(B) My popular Korean star. He is a good doctor in the town.
(C) My friendly Korean teacher. He is a busy man in the town.
( ) 8. 我過去常常透過網路購物平台買東西。
(A) I am going to sell tickets through online stores.
(B) I hate to order sweet things from online scams.
(C) Oh, wow. I used to buy things through online marketplaces, too.
( ) 9. 我只是要去拿（取）我的演唱會票。
(A) I'm just going to write down my concert ticket.
(B) I'm just going to pick up my concert ticket.
(C) I'm just going to buy my concert ticket.
( ) 10. 這張票是我一生中最貴的票！
(A) It was the most expensive ticket of my life!
(B) It was the most popular ticket of my school.
(C) It was the cheapest ticket of my life.
