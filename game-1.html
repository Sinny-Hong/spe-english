代名詞-數量不定代名詞 (Quantifiers)

班級：__________ 姓名：__________

💡 重點：數量代名詞要看名詞是「可數」還是「不可數」

可數名詞專用

one / two / each / many / both

皆可搭配

some / most / all

不可數名詞專用

much (如: water, money)

LEVEL 1 暖身判讀：配對正確嗎？ (正確變 ◯，錯誤變 ✕)
(    ) 1. Many of the water is clean.
(    ) 2. Some of the students are playing games.
(    ) 3. Much of the apples are sweet.
LEVEL 2 進階挑戰：點選字卡放入正確的空格中
🖐️ 選取字卡：

1. There is too ________ water in the glass.

杯子裡有太多的水。

2. He has two cats. ________ of them are very cute.

他有兩隻貓。牠們兩隻都很可愛。

3. I have five books. ________ of them has a red cover.

我有五本書。其中的每一本都有紅色書皮。

LEVEL 3 綜合選擇題：選出最正確的答案

1. (      ) ______ of the money is on the desk.

2. (      ) I bought ten pens. ______ of them are blue.

3. (      ) ______ of the students like to eat apples.

🔑 本講義教師解答：【Level 1】1. ✕   2. ◯   3. ✕   【Level 2】1. much   2. both   3. each   【Level 3】1. (C)   2. (B)   3. (B)
